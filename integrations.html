"Integration with Leading POS Systems for Seamless Cannabis Management"

Bleaum offers seamless integrations with a variety of third-party POS systems, ensuring a unified and streamlined experience for managing orders, inventory, and more. We currently support integration with several leading systems, including Growflow, Cova, Greenbits, Indicaonline, METRC, LightSpeed, and Moneris.

Supported Systems

・ Growflow

Growflow, founded in 2016 and recently purchased by Dama Financial, is trusted by over 1,498 cannabis cultivators, processors, distributors, and retailers. With approximately 8% of the market share in the United States, Growflow has processed over 4 million transactions and processed over $2B. Growflow's platform saves an average of 43 hours each week and is designed to streamline operations, improve customer satisfaction and increase efficiency.


・ Greenbits

Greenbits, founded in 2014, is purchased by Dutchie and has approximately 9% of the market share in the United States. Greenbits is a platform that empowers cannabis retailers with an easy-to-manage platform. Greenbits matches the unique workflows and business demands of cannabis retailers like seed-to-sale tracking, faster checkout lines, customer sales limits, and complex inventory reporting.

・ METRC

METRC, founded in 2013, provides marijuana tracking and reporting systems to support the enforcement of regulations related to the legalization, production, and distribution of cannabis. METRC is the most trusted and experienced provider of cannabis regulatory systems in the United States. The platform is contracted by state regulators and committed to a secure and credible regulatory environment.

・ Indica Online

Indica Online, founded in 2011, has approximately 6% of the market shre in the United States. Indica Online is a POS system designed for cannabis dispensaries that is fully scalable and tackles the entire business operations. The platform manages compliance and efficiency while you focus on customer satisfaction and retention.

・ Cova

Cova, founded in 2016, has approximately 14% of the market share in North America. Cova's cannabis dispensary POS is a full-featured tablet-based point-of-sale system that enables your staff to deliver a fast, compliant, and personalized shopping experience from anywhere in the store. Our award-winning, intuitive design guarantees ease-of-use and quick onboarding of staff in just 30 minutes

Founded in 2000, Moneris is Canada's leading financial technology company specializing in payment processing. With a focus on helping businesses thrive, Moneris offers a range of solutions for in-store, online, and mobile transactions. At Bleaum, we understand the importance of seamless integration, which is why we are proud to offer full integration with Moneris for our customers' payment processing needs.
