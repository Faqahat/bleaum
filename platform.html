"Bleaum To Life"

Welcome to the heart of our business - the Bleaum Platform. Our platform is designed to provide cannabis dispensaries with everything they need to create a professional and user-friendly e-commerce store, fully compliant with all laws and regulations. Whether you're just getting started or looking to take your existing business to the next level, the Bleaum Platform has the tools and support you need to succeed.

One of the key features of our platform is our online store. Our platform offers a powerful and easy-to-use interface for creating a custom online store. The interface is intuitive and user-friendly, making it easy for you to set up and manage your store. You'll be able to showcase your products, accept payments, and more, all in one place.

Phone Applications

In addition to our online store, we also offer IOS and Android Application. Our IOS and Android applications offer the same features as our online store but in a mobile format, allowing you to manage your store on the go from your iPhone or iPad or Android devices. The applications are available for download on the Appstore and Google Playstore.


Android App:

  • Our Android application brings the convenience of our online store to your mobile device. You can shop, track orders, and access customer support all from the palm of your hand. The application is available for download on the Google Playstore and is compatible with all Android devices. The UI is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate, allowing customers to quickly find what they need and make purchases on the go.

Keyfeatures

Promotions and deals

Keep your customers informed about current deals and promotions by creating a dedicated deals page on your store. It can include information about sales, discounts, and other offers currently available.



Brand focus

Allow customers to easily browse and discover products from their favorite brands by creating a dedicated brands page on your store. This page can include information about the brands you carry, including product listings and other relevant details.



Loyalty and referral program

Reward your customers for their purchases and encourage them to refer others to your store by implementing a loyalty points system and referral program.



Third-party login

Make it easy for customers to sign up for an account and start shopping by allowing them to log in using their Google account.



Live customer support

Provide customers with the support they need by offering live chat customer support. This can be used to answer questions, troubleshoot issues, and provide guidance on the use of the store.



Multi-location support

Our platform can handle multiple locations for store owners to easily manage and track their sales, inventory and customers. Integration with industry-leading POS systems: With integration support for top Point of Sale systems, store owners can seamlessly track and manage their inventory and sales.



User-friendly design

Our platform offers an advance and user-friendly interface with mobile-friendly web design. This allows customers to easily navigate the store and make purchases from any device.



Security

We understand that cannabis is a highly regulated industry, and we take the security of our customers' data very seriously. All transactions on our platform are fully encrypted and protected, and we comply with all laws and regulations related to cannabis commerce.
